# Goal / Loop

## What This Category Means

Goal / Loop patterns define structured ways to pursue larger outcomes through repeated implementation, checking, reflection, and adjustment.

## Why It Matters

AI engineering work often requires iteration. Goal / Loop workflows make that iteration explicit, measurable, and less likely to drift.

## What Will Be Collected Here

- Goal loop templates.
- Stop conditions.
- Checklists.
- Progress reporting formats.
- End-to-end task execution patterns.

## How To Evaluate Entries

Evaluate Goal / Loop patterns by clarity of goal, usefulness of checks, quality of stop conditions, progress visibility, and ability to produce complete outcomes.
