# Subagents

## What This Category Means

Subagents are specialized helper agents used for focused tasks inside a larger workflow, such as research, code review, testing, documentation, or evaluation.

## Why It Matters

Subagents help split complex work into smaller responsibilities while keeping the main workflow organized and easier to reason about.

## What Will Be Collected Here

- Subagent role templates.
- Delegation patterns.
- Coordination rules.
- Quality checks.
- Examples of effective task boundaries.

## How To Evaluate Entries

Evaluate subagents by specialization clarity, output quality, coordination cost, repeatability, and whether they reduce cognitive load.
