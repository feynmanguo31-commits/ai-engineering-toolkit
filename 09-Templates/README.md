# Templates

## What This Category Means

Templates are reusable document, task, evaluation, and workflow structures that make repeated AI engineering work faster and more consistent.

## Why It Matters

Templates reduce blank-page work and help preserve standards across projects, tools, and experiments.

## What Will Be Collected Here

- Evaluation templates.
- Documentation templates.
- Task templates.
- Research templates.
- Implementation planning templates.

## How To Evaluate Entries

Evaluate templates by completeness, ease of adaptation, clarity, maintenance cost, and whether they improve repeated work.
