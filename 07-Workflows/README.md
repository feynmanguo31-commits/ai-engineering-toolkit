# Workflows

## What This Category Means

Workflows are repeatable sequences for completing practical AI engineering tasks from start to finish.

## Why It Matters

Clear workflows make complex work easier to execute, review, improve, and delegate to AI-assisted systems.

## What Will Be Collected Here

- Development workflows.
- Research workflows.
- Testing workflows.
- Review workflows.
- Release and maintenance workflows.

## How To Evaluate Entries

Evaluate workflows by completeness, repeatability, tool requirements, failure handling, time saved, and quality of final output.
