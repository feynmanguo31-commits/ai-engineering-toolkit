# Hooks

## What This Category Means

Hooks are event-driven automation points that run checks, prompts, scripts, or workflows before, during, or after AI-assisted development actions.

## Why It Matters

Hooks can improve quality and safety by making important checks automatic instead of relying on memory.

## What Will Be Collected Here

- Hook ideas.
- Safety checks.
- Workflow triggers.
- Validation patterns.
- Notes on when hooks are helpful or too intrusive.

## How To Evaluate Entries

Evaluate hooks by reliability, timing, clarity of failure messages, reversibility, security impact, and whether they improve real workflows.
