# Prompts

## What This Category Means

Prompts are reusable instructions, request patterns, and interaction structures for guiding AI systems toward useful outputs.

## Why It Matters

Good prompts make AI workflows clearer, more reliable, and easier to improve over time.

## What Will Be Collected Here

- Task prompts.
- Review prompts.
- Research prompts.
- Agent setup prompts.
- Prompt evaluation notes.

## How To Evaluate Entries

Evaluate prompts by clarity, output consistency, required context, failure modes, ease of reuse, and whether they produce verifiably useful results.
